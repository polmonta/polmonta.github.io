ManageState Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

Thank you for choosing ManageState! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

By using ManageState, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use ManageState, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication.
  • Name: Collected for personalization purposes (optional).

b. Property and Financial Data

To enable property management features, you may input:

  • Property details (addresses, tenant information, lease terms).
  • Financial transactions (income, expenses, receipts).
  • Documents (uploaded files such as lease agreements or receipts).

c. Usage Data

We automatically collect the following data to improve the App's performance and user experience:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system version).
  • App usage patterns (e.g., features used, session duration).
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Sentry).

d. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not currently use cookies within the App. However, third-party services (e.g., analytics tools) may use tracking technologies for operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide Services: Enable property management features such as income tracking, expense logging, and document storage.
  • Authenticate and Secure: Verify your identity and secure your account.
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage data to enhance functionality and user experience.
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide assistance.
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data Storage and Security

a. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud-based service provider. Data is encrypted in transit (using TLS/SSL) and at rest.

b. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices, including:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data.
  • Secure authentication powered by Supabase Auth.
  • Regular monitoring and updates to prevent unauthorized access.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use the App at your own risk.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

a. Service Providers

We use third-party services to operate the App, including:

  • Supabase: For backend infrastructure, database, and authentication.
  • Sentry: For error tracking and crash reporting.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (if applicable).

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it to provide their services.

b. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

c. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you of any such changes.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions for data processing (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, contact us at managestateapp@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will permanently delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

ManageState is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located in different countries. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: managestateapp@gmail.com
